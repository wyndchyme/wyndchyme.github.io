About Me

Who am I?

I'm wyndchyme (often known as Dale). I make mods for Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition, along with making some things for the Wii on the side. My favorite part of making things is making them look nice and pretty.

What kind of things do I do?

For Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition, I've worked on texture packs, GUI textures, some audio work, and a little work on fonts as well. Texture work is certainly my strong suit. For the Wii, I mostly design WAD forwarders for homebrew applications. Pixel art is another interest of mine – I made all the icons for this website! I also maintain an archived version of the Minecraft 3DS UniStore for Universal-Updater, along with a modpack for Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition that contains a lot of quality-of-life fixes.

What can I expect to find on this website?

This website has links to all the things I've worked on for the Wii and 3DS along with links to modding tools and a newsletter for Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition modding.

What's my favorite color?

Blue. Can you tell?

Anything else?

Sometimes I eat rocks.
