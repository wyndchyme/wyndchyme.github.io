About Me

Who am I?

I'm wyndchyme (often known as Dale). I make mods for Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition, along with making some things for the Wii on the side.

What kind of things do I do?

For Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition, I've worked on texture packs, GUI textures, some audio work, and a little work on fonts as well. Texture work is certainly my strong suit. For the Wii, I mostly design WAD forwarders for homebrew applications. I also made all the icons for this website!

What can I expect to find on this website?

This website has links to all the things I've worked on for the Wii and 3DS along with links to modding tools and a newsletter for Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition modding.

What's my favorite color?

Blue. Can you tell?
