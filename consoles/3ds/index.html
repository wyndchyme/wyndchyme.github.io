Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition


Modernization MegaPack

mc3ds-modern

A modpack that aims to make the game feel more modern. Contains many quality-of-life mods from many different modders – Certainly my most extensive project.


MegaPack Standalone: Classic Textures

mc3ds-classic-textures

The Classic texture pack included in Modernization MegaPack, released as a standalone mod.


MegaPack Standalone: Achievement Textures

mc3ds-achievements

The new achievement textures included in Modernization MegaPack, released as a standalone mod.


Pink Floyd Painting Pack

mc3ds-pinkfloyd

Replaces the vanilla game's paintings with Pink Floyd album covers. Quite possibly my least necessary mod.

An archive of the Team Proteus UniStore for Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition modifications.
